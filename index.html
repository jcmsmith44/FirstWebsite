My Top 10 NES Titles


Hello! I have created this website as a project for "The Complete Web Developer in 2019:Zero to Mastery" class on Udemy.com by instructor Andrei Neagoie for the project: HTML Challenge. This site will likely never go live on the internet so this disclaimer may seem perhaps a bit contrived. However, for the purposes of authenticity and precaution (because who knows, some of my peers in the class may be hardcore retro gamers), I have decided to include it.

Disclaimer: I am not claiming that these are the best quality NES games of all time or the most popular. These are purely my personal opinions. As this is website is the first step in what I would like to be a new career path and a more fulfilling life, I would like it to have personal meaning to me; something that I can look back upon fondly years from now. Thus, this list reflects my top 10 favorite NES games of all time. One of my favorite hobbies is speedrunning retro video games, so this felt like a natural topic on which to create my first site. I will rank the games on a few factors:

  • Quality - Is the game made well with regard to mechanics, story, and popular appeal?
  • Nostalgia - How much influence did the game have on me as a kid and how much nostalgia does it evoke for me?
  • Difficulty - I enjoy the challenge of difficult games
  • Speedrun - Is it fun to speedrun/watch?

I will be ranking the games relative to each other (1 = best and 10 = worst) for each category, then giving them an overall score. I hope everone enjoys the site and I look forward to your feedback, constructive criticism, and maybe even some retro gaming banter! ;)
